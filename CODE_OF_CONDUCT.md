# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

MjolnirOS contributors are expected to help maintain a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive community.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being considerate and professional in discussions and reviews.
- Welcoming questions and contributions from people with different experience levels.
- Giving actionable feedback focused on the work rather than the person.
- Respecting privacy, security boundaries, and differing viewpoints.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and helping resolve disagreements constructively.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, deliberate disruption, publishing another person's private information, or other conduct inappropriate for a professional open-source project.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, and other public spaces associated with MjolnirOS. It also applies when someone is representing the project in another community.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/bhavukm007). Do not disclose sensitive personal information in a public issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these standards and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will be reviewed as promptly, fairly, and confidentially as practical.
